PROJECT DRAFT

MindTrack – Intelligent Mental Healthcare Daily Routine App



Project Overview
MindTrack is a mobile application that helps users monitor their mood, track daily
habits, and improve mental well-being. The app combines computer science and
psychology by providing personalized feedback, visualizing trends, and
encouraging positive routines.

Problem Statement:
Many people struggle with stress, irregular habits, and low self-awareness of their
mental health. Existing apps often track only mood or habits and provide little
guidance. MindTrack fills this gap by tracking both and giving insights for
improvement.

Main Features:
1. Smart Medication Management
2. AI Health Assistant (Big Differentiator)
3. Routine + Habit Builder
4. Health Metrics Dashboard
5. Emergency Features (Very Valuable)
6. Appointment & Doctor Management
7. Mental Health Support
8. Nutrition & Hydration Tracker
9. Gamification (Keeps Users Engaged)
10. Family / Caregiver Mode
11. Offline Mode + Sync
12. Data Export & Reports

Interdisciplinary Aspect:
• Computer Science: app development, database, data visualization
• Psychology: habit formation, mood analysis, behavioral insights

Benefits:
• Helps users track and understand their mood and habits
• Encourages positive behavior and routine
• Provides data-driven insights and self-improvement guidance

Technologies will be used:
Kotlin (back-end)
Jetpack compose (front-end)
Firebase (database and backend)
